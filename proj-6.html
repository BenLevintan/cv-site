Network Traffic Analyzer

Created a web-based utility for network engineers to upload packet capture (PCAP) files and instantly receive visualizations of traffic flows, protocol distributions, and potential anomalies.

Key Features

  • Backend packet parsing utilizing PyShark
  • Interactive frontend charts and tables
  • Identification of top talkers and connections
  • Exportable PDF reporting

Tech Stack
